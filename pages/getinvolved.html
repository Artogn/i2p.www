Get involved

To get involved, please feel free to join us on the #i2p IRC channel (on irc.freenode.net, or within I2P on irc.duck.i2p or irc.baffled.i2p). In addition, we have a mailing list and meetings are held every Tuesday at 9PM GMT on IRC (with meeting logs kept online)

If you're interested in joining our team, please get in touch as we're always looking for eager contributors!
