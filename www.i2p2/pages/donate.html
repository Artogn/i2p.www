As of December 2010, eche|on has been running a Bitcoin account for the I2P project. If you'd like to donate using Bitcoin, just transfer your desired amount of coins to the account 1HkJCceXf7of1sTNRVJbXiZHfDTLL71Siy and leave eche|on a note if you'd like your donation to be mentioned on the I2P webpage.

If you'd like to donate via snail mail, send an email to echelon@i2pmail.org and you'll receive an email with instructions detailing how to proceed.
